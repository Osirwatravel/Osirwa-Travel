Welcome to Osirwa Travel and Safaris

Your trusted partner for unforgettable travel experiences in Tanzania and beyond.

We are a proud tourism company offering authentic and unique safari experiences across Tanzania and East Africa. Our focus is on connecting visitors with the natural heritage, rich cultures, and breathtaking landscapes of Africa. With many years of experience in the tourism industry, our team of tour guides are professional, friendly, and deeply knowledgeable about the destinations we serve.

We warmly welcome you — every journey with us is safe, exciting, and truly unforgettable.

About Us

Osirwa Travel and Safaris derived its name from the deep bond between people and nature.

We specialize in crafting authentic African experiences that inspire joy, peace, and unforgettable memories. As a proudly East African travel and tourism company, we are dedicated to offering safe, peaceful, and fulfilling journeys across the region’s most stunning landscapes.

Our experienced team combines expert local knowledge of natural attractions, cultural heritage, and diverse wildlife to deliver meaningful travel experiences that connect you to the true spirit of Africa.

Mission:

To provide unforgettable, sustainable, and culturally rich travel experiences across Africa, connecting travelers with nature, wildlife, and local communities through personalized and responsible safari adventures.

Vision:

To be a leading African travel and safari company, inspiring global travelers through exceptional service, conservation-focused tourism, and authentic African experiences.
